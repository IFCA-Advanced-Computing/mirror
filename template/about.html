Welcome to the public repository server of the Advanced Computing and e-Science research group at the Institute of Physics of Cantabria (IFCA) a joint center of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC) and the University of Cantabria. This server hosts mirrors for many Open Source projects, in particular it hosts many mirrors for popular linux distributions.

The server is physically located in Santander (Cantabria), in the nothern part of Spain. It is connected to the Internet through a 10Gbps connection to the Spanish National Research and Education Network (RedIRIS). The current server hardware is the following:

  • HP DL360p G8 with Intel(R) Xeon(R) CPUs E5-2640 (2.50GHz, 2 sockets with 12 cores + 12 SMT threads)

The administrators of the server can be reached at cloud.admin@ifca.unican.es. {% endblock %}
