Welcome to the public repository server of the Advanced Computing and e-Science research group at the Institute of Physics of Cantabria (IFCA) a joint center of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC) and the University of Cantabria. This server hosts mirrors for several Open Source projects, in particular for popular linux distributions. We are open to mirror any intersting project upon request.

The server is physically located in Santander (Cantabria), in the northern part of Spain. It is connected to the Internet through a 10Gbps connection via the Spanish National Research and Education Network (RedIRIS). The current server hardware is the following:

  • ThinkServer TD350
  • Intel(R) Xeon(R) CPU E5-2640 v3 2.60GHz (8 cores, 16 SMT threads)
  • 8GB RAM
  • 77 TB disk
  • 2 x 1Gbit Ethernet

The administrators of the server can be reached at computing.support@ifca.unican.es.
